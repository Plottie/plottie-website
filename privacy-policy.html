Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Introduction

Plottie ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and related services.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Email address (for account creation and communication)
  • Name (optional, for personalisation)
  • Location data (for weather integration and local recommendations)

Usage Information

  • Plant photos you upload for identification
  • Garden planning data and preferences
  • Care schedules and reminders you set
  • App usage patterns and feature interactions

Device Information

  • Device type and operating system
  • App version and crash reports
  • IP address and general location

How We Use Your Information

  • Provide plant identification and care recommendations
  • Personalise your gardening experience
  • Send notifications and reminders
  • Improve our app and services
  • Provide customer support
  • Ensure app security and prevent fraud

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with:

  • Plant identification service providers (for AI analysis)
  • Weather service providers (for local weather data)
  • Cloud storage providers (for data backup)
  • Analytics services (for app improvement)

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information, including encryption and secure data storage practices.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete your account and data
  • Opt out of marketing communications
  • Export your data

Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. You can delete your account at any time.

Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy in the app.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy@plottie.app
